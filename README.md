## 👋 About Me

Hi, I'm **Phong Ha** — a **Junior UI/UX Designer** and **Information Technology student** from Ho Chi Minh City.

I enjoy designing digital products that are not only visually clean, but also **easy to understand, scalable, and practical for real development**. My work sits at the intersection of **UI/UX design, product thinking, business analysis, and frontend development**.

Currently, I am working as a **Junior UI/UX Designer at SALTO Vietnam**, where I research user behavior, design user-centered interfaces, create wireframes/prototypes/design systems, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to improve digital product experiences.

```txt
My design mindset:
User needs first  →  Clear user flows  →  Consistent UI systems  →  Practical implementation
```
