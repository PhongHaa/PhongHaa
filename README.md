## ✨ About Me

I'm **Phong Ha**, a designer and technology learner who enjoys connecting **UI/UX design**, **project thinking**, and **software development** to create digital products that are clear, useful, and easy to use.

I care about building interfaces that are not only visually clean, but also logical, scalable, and friendly for both users and development teams. My long-term goal is to grow into a well-rounded IT professional who can contribute to meaningful products and sustainable business value.

- 🎨 I enjoy designing user flows, wireframes, design systems, dashboards, and interactive prototypes.
- 💻 I am continuously improving my frontend and software development skills.
- 📌 I like working with structured processes, clear documentation, and product-oriented thinking.
- 🚀 I believe good products come from the balance between user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility.
