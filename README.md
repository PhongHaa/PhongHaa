## 👋 About Me

Hi, I'm **Phong Ha** — a **UI/UX Designer** and **Frontend Developer** from Ho Chi Minh City.

I design and build digital products that are not only visually clean, but also **easy to understand, scalable, responsive, and practical for real development**. My work sits at the intersection of **UI/UX design, frontend development, product thinking, and business analysis**.

Currently, I work as a **UI/UX Designer & Frontend Developer**, creating user-centered interfaces, wireframes, prototypes, design systems, and responsive frontend experiences. I enjoy collaborating with teams to turn product ideas into clear user flows, polished UI, and usable digital products.

```txt
My workflow:
User needs  →  UX flow  →  UI system  →  Prototype  →  Frontend implementation
```
